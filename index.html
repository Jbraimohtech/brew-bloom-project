Our Story:

Imagine a cozy little coffee shop tucked away on a side street, where the scent of freshly ground beans lures people in from the chilly air outside. Soft jazz hums in the background, and warm, dim lights cast a gentle glow on the eclectic furniture-vintage armchairs, mismatched tables, a couch that looks like it has its own stories to tell.

Regulars know the barista by name, a cheerful soul who always has a new coffee recommendation based on the weather or the customer's mood. Each drink here is crafted width intention, from the way the milk is steamed to the latte art carefully poured on top. A heart, a leaf, sometimes a flower-tiny, ephemeral art for a small moment of joy.

There's a corner by the window where a writer sets up every morning with a laptop and a notebook. Across the room, an old man reads the paper, sipping black coffee as he watches the world go by. Friends gather at the big wooden table, sharing stories over pastries that are backed fresh by a local baker and delivered each morning, still warm.

On the walls are postcards and pictures left by travelers, giving glimpses of places far beyond this little shop. It's as if each cup of coffee holds a piece of someone's story, shared in quite companionship with everyone else who sits here. This coffee shop isn't just a place for caffeine; it's a little world of it's own, where time slows, connections are formed, and everyday moments feel a little more meaningful.

Our Menu:

Espresso

Also known as a short black, an espresso is a single shot of coffee. No extra hot water added, resulting in an intense and flavoursome drink. An espresso shot, which forms the basis of many of the other drinks to follow, is produced by forcing hot water through finely ground coffee beans.

Price: $50

Doppio

A Doppio is a double shot of espresso with no added hot water or milk. This results in 60 ml of coffee. Doppio means 'double' in Italian.

Price: $44.99

Short Macchiato

A short macchiato is served in a small glass consisting of a single espresso shot then filled with creamy steamed milk and finished with a small layer of foam.

Price: $55

Long Macchiato

A long macchiato is similar to a shot macchiato, except that it contains a double shot (around 60ml) of coffee.

Price: $50

Latte

A latte is a coffee espresso shot filled with steamed milk and with a layer of foamed mil crema.

Price: $59.99

Mezzo Mezzo(Piccolo)

A mezzo mezzo (also known as a piccolo) is a single espresso shot in a small latte glass, which is then filled with steamed milk.

Price: $40
